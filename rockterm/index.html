RockTerm

A modern terminal emulator with SSH, Telnet, and serial console support — plus integrated AI assistance.

Built for network engineers and system administrators who spend their day in the terminal. RockTerm combines fast, reliable SSH, Telnet, and serial connections with AI-powered assistance that understands your infrastructure context. Diagnose issues faster, automate repetitive tasks, and manage connections efficiently.

Features

⌨

AI-Powered Terminal

Get contextual AI assistance right inside your terminal session. Ask questions about command output, get help with configuration syntax, or troubleshoot errors — without leaving your workflow.

⚡

Fast & Native

Built with native UI toolkits for responsive performance. No Electron, no web views. Launches fast, stays fast.

☰

Connection Management

Organize SSH, Telnet, and serial connections with saved profiles. Group hosts by environment, project, or role. Connect with one click.

🔒

Multi-Protocol Support

SSH, Telnet, and serial console in one application. SSH key management, agent forwarding, and configurable serial port settings. Your credentials and sessions stay on your machine.

▦

Professional Interface

Clean, modern dark interface designed for extended use. Multiple tabbed sessions, customizable appearance, and a distraction-free layout.

⇩

Microsoft Store Distribution

Install and update through the Microsoft Store. Clean installation, automatic updates, and easy licensing.
