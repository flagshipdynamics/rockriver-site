Free Trial and Licensing

RockWiFi™ Pro Support

Home › RockWiFi Pro › Support › Free Trial and Licensing

Free Trial

RockWiFi Pro includes a free trial with full functionality. Every feature — surveys, heatmaps, diagnostics, reports, UltraScan, Wi-Fi 7 detection — works during the trial. No features are locked or restricted.

No Account Required

You don't need to create an account, provide an email, or sign up for anything. Download from the Microsoft Store and start using it immediately.

Monthly Subscription

After the trial, RockWiFi Pro is a monthly subscription through the Microsoft Store. Cancel anytime. No long-term contract.

What You Get

Your subscription includes all features and all future updates:

  • Complete Wi-Fi survey and heatmap tools
  • All network diagnostic tools (Connection Check, Target Test, DNS Compare, Latency Monitor)
  • UltraScan AP discovery
  • Channel Congestion analysis
  • Wi-Fi 7 and 6 GHz features
  • Flight Recorder
  • Professional reporting and CSV export
  • AP Locator
  • All future feature updates

No Cloud, No Data Collection

Your license is managed by the Microsoft Store. We don't collect your data, require cloud connectivity, or track usage. Your surveys and network data never leave your machine.

Enterprise / Volume Licensing

For volume purchases, contact us at the email on our website.

Refund Policy

Refunds follow the Microsoft Store refund policy. If you're not satisfied, request a refund through the Microsoft Store.
