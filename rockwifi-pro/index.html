What RockWiFi Pro Does

📡

Dashboard

Live Wi-Fi status at a glance — SSID, signal strength (% and dBm), band, channel, Wi-Fi standard (Wi-Fi 7/6/5/4), MLO status, channel width, and a scrolling signal chart. Quick-launch buttons for every tool.

🗺

Wi-Fi Survey & Heatmaps

Click-to-sample heatmaps on imported floor plans or OpenStreetMap tiles. Kriging interpolation, multi-sample clustering, and a guided tour mode for consistent coverage walks.

🔭

AP Locator

Hot/cold signal gauge to physically walk to any access point. Pin positions on a floor plan for trilateration. BSS metrics including station count, channel utilization, and Wi-Fi standard detection. Channel congestion chart shows co-channel interference at a glance.

🔌

Connection Check

Full connectivity diagnostic — adapter, gateway, internet, DNS, latency, jitter, and packet loss. One click to find out exactly where the connection breaks.

🎯

Target Test

Probe a specific host or port with ping, TCP connect, HTTP, traceroute, and TLS certificate inspection. Know whether the problem is the network or the service.

📚

DNS Compare

Query multiple DNS resolvers side-by-side — system, Google, Cloudflare, Quad9, or custom — and compare results. Spot DNS-level filtering, hijacking, or stale records instantly.

📈

Latency Monitor

Continuous ping chart over time with jitter, loss, and percentile stats. Leave it running during a meeting and come back with proof of intermittent drops.

⏺

Flight Recorder

Background logging of connectivity metrics for post-incident analysis. Capture what happened while you were away — latency spikes, packet loss bursts, DNS failures.

📄

Reports & History

Browse past results, export HTML or text reports for client deliverables. Built-in findings engine with per-tool scoring, capability analysis, and actionable recommendations. Professional output without extra formatting work.

📡

UltraScan

Continuous background AP scanner tracking all visible access points over time. Signal history, stability analysis, 10 sort modes, and CSV/HTML export for thorough site documentation.

📶

Channel Congestion

Full spectrum visualization for 2.4, 5, and 6 GHz bands. Per-channel scoring, UNII band labeling, channel recommendations, and AP detail cards to identify interference sources.

Wi-Fi 7 Ready

Wi-Fi 7 is rolling out across enterprise and consumer hardware. RockWiFi Pro detects and reports on Wi-Fi 7 capabilities so you can assess readiness, validate deployments, and document upgrade paths — all from a single tool.

6 GHz Band Detection

Full visibility into the 6 GHz spectrum — UNII-5 through UNII-8, covering 1200 MHz of new spectrum. See which access points are broadcasting on 6 GHz and evaluate coverage across all three bands.

320 MHz Channel Width

Detect and report on 320 MHz channel widths introduced by Wi-Fi 7. Identify APs operating at maximum throughput and spot channel width mismatches across your deployment.

Multi-Link Operation (MLO)

Detect MLO-capable access points and report on multi-link status. See which APs are aggregating across bands for higher throughput and lower latency.

Wi-Fi 7 Readiness Scoring

Reports include a Wi-Fi 7 readiness score based on detected capabilities. Know at a glance how prepared a site is for the next generation of wireless.

Upgrade Path Recommendations

The findings engine identifies APs that would benefit from Wi-Fi 7 upgrades and flags hardware still running older standards. Clear, actionable recommendations for client proposals.

How People Use RockWiFi Pro
