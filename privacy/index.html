Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 2026

This privacy policy describes how Rock River Research, LLC ("we," "us," or "our") handles information in connection with RockTerm and our website.

Information We Collect

RockTerm does not collect personal data. We do not use telemetry, usage analytics, or tracking of any kind. The software does not phone home, transmit diagnostics, or gather behavioral data.

RockTerm includes optional AI integration features that, when explicitly initiated by the user, may send terminal context to a third-party AI provider (Anthropic's Claude API) to generate responses. No data is sent to any external service unless you explicitly invoke the AI assistant. You are always in control of when and whether AI features are used.

Microsoft Store

RockTerm is distributed through the Microsoft Store. Purchases, licensing, and account management are handled entirely by Microsoft. Microsoft collects payment information, account details, and other data in accordance with their own privacy policy. Rock River Research does not receive or store your payment details, Microsoft account information, or billing data.

Data Storage

All user data — including SSH connection profiles, application settings, and session history — is stored locally on your machine. Nothing is transmitted to Rock River Research servers. We do not operate servers that receive, store, or process your data.

Third-Party Services

When you use RockTerm's AI features, the data you send to the AI assistant is transmitted to the third-party AI provider (currently Anthropic). That data is governed by the AI provider's own privacy policy and terms of service. Rock River Research does not control how the AI provider processes your data.

You can disable AI features entirely in RockTerm's settings. When AI features are disabled, no data is sent to any third-party service.

Children's Privacy

RockTerm is not designed for or directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at contact@rockriverresearch.com.
