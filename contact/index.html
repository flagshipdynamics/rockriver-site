What Support Covers

Support covers our products — installation, configuration, features, and bugs across RockWiFi Pro, RockWiFi Essentials, and RockTerm. We’re happy to help you get connected and working. Support does not extend to:

  • Configuration of your network devices, routers, access points, or servers
  • Issues with third-party hardware, operating systems, or network infrastructure
  • AI feature billing (RockTerm), which is handled directly by your AI provider under your own API key
