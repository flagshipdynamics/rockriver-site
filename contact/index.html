What Support Covers

Support covers RockTerm itself — installation, configuration, features, and bugs. We’re happy to help you get connected and working. Support does not extend to:

  • Configuration of your network devices, servers, or SSH hosts
  • Issues with third-party SSH servers or operating systems
  • AI feature billing, which is handled directly by your AI provider (Anthropic or OpenAI) under your own API key
