Editions: Essentials and Pro

RockSolid Money Support

Home › RockSolid Money › Support › Editions: Essentials and Pro

RockSolid Money comes in two editions built from the same application.

Essentials

The complete import engine and day-to-day money management: every account type, the register, reconciliation, budgets, cash flow, bills & income, goals, including full investment and crypto import with cost basis, net worth tracking, and core reports.

Pro

Everything in Essentials, plus the advanced analysis layer: advanced reporting, tax reporting with per-lot capital gains (Form 8949-ready long/short separation), investment performance and allocation analysis, the document vault, batch import and additional institutional statement formats, multi-currency accounts, and audit-grade exports.

What is never gated

Correctness and safety are identical in both editions — import accuracy, duplicate detection, sign-proving, evidence, rollback, backups, and every data-integrity rule. The editions differ in breadth of analysis, never in how carefully your money is handled. A cheaper edition that guessed more would not be a smaller product; it would be a worse one, and we don't ship it.
