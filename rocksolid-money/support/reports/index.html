RockSolid Money™ Support

Home › RockSolid Money › Support › Reports

Analyze > Reports is a library of built reports — pick one from the left, set the filters, read the result. Nothing to configure before it's useful.

The library

Both editions include the core set: Spending by Category, Spending by Payee, Income & Expenses, Monthly Cash Flow, Account Balances, Transaction Detail, and Budget vs. Actual.

Pro adds the analysis and tax layer: Net Worth History, Asset Allocation, Investment Income, Capital Gains, Crypto Income, Crypto Realized Gains, Tax Category Summary, and Tax Lot Detail. An edition without a report simply doesn't list it — you never see a button that upsells instead of running.

Favorites

Star a report (★) and it pins to a Favorites group at the top of the library.

Drill-down

Report rows are backed by real transactions — double-click a row (or a total) to open the transactions behind the number. A figure you can't decompose is a figure you can't trust; every one here decomposes.

Print and export

Every report prints, and exports as CSV (for a spreadsheet) or JSON (for anything else). Exports contain exactly what the report shows.
