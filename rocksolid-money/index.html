What RockSolid Money™ Does

Import From Any Bank

CSV, OFX, QFX, and QIF from any bank or brokerage. Auto-detects column layout, validates against running balances, and proposes changes for your review.

All Your Accounts

Checking, savings, credit cards, loans, and investment accounts in one unified ledger.

Transactions

Categories, payees, memos, tags, and splits. Transfers matched automatically. Re-imports never create duplicates.

Budgets & Cash Flow

Set budgets by category and track actual spending. Income and expenses summarized by month with recurring items projected forward.

Investments & Net Worth

Holdings tracked lot by lot with cost basis. Net worth computed from all assets and liabilities, with history over time.

Reports

Spending, income, cash flow, balances, and net worth. Every total drills through to the transactions behind it.

Files are evidence, not transactions

Each imported row is validated and matched against existing records before it becomes a proposal. Input that can't be interpreted unambiguously is reported, not guessed at.

Format detection

Unrecognized CSVs get a proposed column mapping, sign convention tested against the running balance, and date format inferred from the data. Confirm once and reuse the profile.

Re-import safety

Same statement imported twice does not create duplicates. Fields you've edited are never overwritten. Commits are atomic and can be rolled back.

When basic budgeting isn't enough

Pro adds advanced analysis for investments, taxes, and financial planning — built on the same ledger and import engine.

Tax Center

Per-lot capital gains with long/short-term separation. Form 8949 ready. Retirement accounts excluded from taxable totals.

Investment Performance

Realized and unrealized gains from the same per-lot records the tax reports use. One set of numbers, consistent everywhere.

Asset Allocation

Portfolio composition by asset class and account, consolidated across every investment account in the file.

Advanced Reports

Custom date ranges, comparison periods, and drill-through from any total to the transactions behind it.

Spending Insights

Observations about patterns, recurring charges, and unusual activity. Suggestions for review — never automatic changes.

Document Vault

Attach statements, receipts, and tax documents to the accounts and transactions they relate to.
