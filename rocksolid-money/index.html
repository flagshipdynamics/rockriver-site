Personal finance the way it used to be — only better.

Microsoft Money and Quicken taught a generation to manage money on their own computer. Then everything moved to the cloud, wrapped in subscriptions and built to mine your transactions. RockSolid Money brings it home: every account, every transaction, every investment lives in a local file you own. It imports the thousands of ways banks export data, treats those files as evidence to be verified — never as gospel — and only ever proposes changes you approve.

What RockSolid Money Does

Import From Any Bank

The heart of the app. Drop in a CSV, OFX, QFX, or QIF from any bank, card, or brokerage and RockSolid Money learns the format, verifies the signs against your running balance, and proposes clean transactions. Imported files are treated as evidence — nothing touches your ledger until you approve it.

All Your Accounts

Checking, savings, credit cards, loans, cash, and investment accounts side by side. One consolidated picture of everything you own and owe — kept in a single local file, not scattered across bank logins.

Transactions & Transfers

A fast register with categories, payees, memos, tags, and splits. Transfers between your own accounts are matched automatically, and re-importing a statement never silently duplicates what's already there.

Budgets

Set category budgets and watch actual spending fill them in as transactions land. See where the money went this month, and where it's on track to go next — without any guesswork about the numbers.

Cash Flow

Money in versus money out, month over month. Recurring income and expenses are detected and projected forward so you can see a low balance coming before it becomes an overdraft.

Bills & Reminders

Track recurring bills and subscriptions, get reminded before they're due, and spot the creeping charges you forgot you signed up for. Your calendar of obligations, in one place.

Investments & Holdings

Import brokerage activity and track holdings lot by lot with accurate cost basis. Stocks, ETFs, bonds, and crypto are handled with per-lot precision — because a portfolio that's off by a rounding error isn't a portfolio you can trust.

Net Worth Over Time

Every asset weighed against every liability, tracked as a single line that (hopefully) climbs. Watch the trend across months and years and see the effect of paying down debt or growing savings.

Reports

Spending by category, income by source, net worth trend, and account activity — clear reports that answer real questions about your money. Everything is computed from your ledger, so the numbers always reconcile.

Goes Deeper When You Need It

Tax Center

Capital gains computed per lot with long-term and short-term split out the way Form 8949 expects. Retirement accounts are excluded from taxable gains, and cost basis is never fabricated — if a lot's basis is unknown, it says so instead of inventing a number.

Investment Performance

See how holdings have performed with realized and unrealized gains, using the same per-lot accounting the tax reports rely on. One source of truth — your performance numbers and your tax numbers can't disagree.

Asset Allocation

Break your portfolio down by asset class and account so you can see concentration and drift at a glance. Know what you actually hold, across every account, without a spreadsheet.

Loans & Debt

Mortgages, auto loans, and other debts tracked alongside your assets, so your net worth reflects the whole picture — not just the balances that make you feel good.

Insights

Plain-language observations about your spending, recurring charges, and unusual activity — suggestions you can act on, never automated decisions about your money. The app can point things out; you stay in charge.

Document Vault

Keep statements, receipts, and tax documents attached to the transactions and accounts they belong to. Your financial paperwork, organized and searchable, stored locally with everything else.

Imports You Can Actually Trust

Files Are Evidence, Not Truth

An imported statement is a claim about what happened — not a transaction. RockSolid Money interprets, matches, and validates every row, and only ever proposes ledger changes you review and approve. Ambiguous input fails safe instead of quietly guessing wrong.

It Learns Your Bank's Format

Unrecognized CSV? The app suggests a column mapping, proves the sign convention against your running balance ("verified against 143 balances," not a checkbox), and figures out the date format from the data itself. Confirm it once and the next import just works.

Never Duplicates, Never Overwrites

Re-import the same statement and nothing doubles up. Your edits — categories, memos, payees, reconciliation status — are never silently overwritten. Every commit is atomic, with a clean path to roll it back.

How People Use RockSolid Money

"I just switched banks."

Export the last few years from the old bank and drop the file in. RockSolid Money maps the columns, verifies the signs against your balances, and brings your whole history over — then the new bank's exports import the same way from then on.

"I need to reconcile this statement."

Import the month's activity, match it against what's already in your register, and clear each transaction against the statement balance. Duplicates are caught automatically, and anything ambiguous is flagged for you rather than guessed.

"It's tax season."

Pull realized capital gains split into long-term and short-term, one line per lot, with retirement accounts left out of the taxable total. Attach the brokerage 1099s in the document vault and hand your accountant a clean picture.

Built the RockSolid Way

Local-First, Always

Your accounts, transactions, and documents live in a file on your machine. No cloud sync, no external servers holding your financial life.

No Subscription

One-time purchase. The license is yours. No annual renewals, no paywalled features you already paid for, no surprise charges.

No Telemetry

RockSolid Money does not track you, does not phone home, and does not sell — or even see — your transactions. That's the whole point.

Accurate to the Penny

Money is handled with exact decimal arithmetic, never floating point. Investment lots, multipliers, and cost basis are computed the careful way, so the totals reconcile every time.

You Stay in Control

The app can suggest, match, and flag — but it never decides financial truth for you. Every proposed change is yours to approve, edit, or reject.

Direct Support

Email the people who build the product. No ticket queues, no chatbots, no support tiers — just the developers.
