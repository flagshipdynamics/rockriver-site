About Rock River Research

Rock River Research is a software company focused on building professional-grade tools for technical practitioners — network engineers, system administrators, and infrastructure teams.

We started with a straightforward observation: the tools that technical professionals depend on every day are often bloated, overengineered, or built by people who don't actually use them. We take a different approach. We build software we use ourselves, we ship when it's ready, and we iterate based on how the tools perform in real workflows — not on feature checklists.

Our first product, RockTerm, is a Windows-native terminal emulator with SSH, Telnet, and serial console support, plus opt-in AI assistance. It represents our approach: take a tool that every network professional uses daily, and make it meaningfully better without adding complexity.

We're building a portfolio of focused tools over time — each one designed to solve a specific class of problem well. No platform plays. No feature bloat. Just useful software for people who do real work.
