About Rock River Research

An American software company building focused Windows desktop applications across networking, terminal access, and personal finance.

Who We Are

Rock River Research is a software company that builds professional Windows desktop applications. We started with Wi-Fi analysis tools for network engineers, expanded into terminal emulation, and are now building personal finance software.

Every product follows the same approach: solve a real problem, keep data local, price it clearly, and support it directly. We don’t build platforms or chase subscriptions for their own sake. We build tools that do specific jobs well.

What We Build

Our product line spans three areas:

  • Network analysis — RockWiFi™ Pro and RockWiFi™ Essentials provide Wi-Fi site surveys, channel congestion analysis, AP locating, heatmaps, and network diagnostics for IT professionals.
  • Terminal access — RockTerm™ is a Windows-native terminal emulator with SSH, Telnet, serial console, RDP, file transfer, scripting, and 110+ features for network engineers.
  • Personal finance — RockSolid Money™ is a local-first finance app that replaces Microsoft Money and Quicken with modern account tracking, budgets, cash flow, investments, and net worth reporting. Launching July 31, 2026.

How We Work

Built by Practitioners

We build tools we use ourselves, for work we actually do. Real workflows drive every decision.

Local-First Data

Everything runs on your machine. No telemetry, no cloud accounts, no license servers. Your data stays yours.

Direct Support

When you reach out, you get the people who built the software—not a ticket queue or chatbot.

American-Made

Rock River Research, LLC is incorporated in the State of Wyoming. Our software is designed, built, and supported entirely by U.S.-based developers. When you reach out for help, you hear back from a real person in the United States.
